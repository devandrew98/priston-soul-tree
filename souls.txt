¶ Souls System
You've just started on the server and suddenly spot a strange drop on the ground: a Soul . You might wonder: What's that for? Let's break down the Souls system so you can make the most of it.

¶ What Are Souls For?
During your leveling journey, you'll encounter various Souls of different attributes and rarities. But what are they for?
Souls are used to enhance your Soul Tree (accessed by pressing the Y key ). In the Soul Tree, you can organize your Souls according to your needs. Since the optimal distribution of Souls is highly personal, this guide won’t suggest a “best way” to set up your tree. Instead, it's meant to be customized according to your preferences and playstyle.

¶ Unlocking and Using Soul Slots
¶ Early Levels (Up to Level 80)
At the start, introductory quests help you get familiar with the Souls system. Up to level 80 , you will unlock three initial slots:

1 Attack Slot 
1 Defense Slot 
1 Wildcard Slot (any type of Soul)

¶ Level 80 and Beyond
From level 80 onwards , you will earn 3 Soul Points per level . You can use these points to:

Open new Soul Slots 
Upgrade the level of existing slots 

¶ Types of Souls and Slots
Souls are divided into four categories:

Attack 
Defense 
Support 
PvP 

The Soul Tree can accommodate the following slots:

12 Attack Slots 
13 Defense Slots 
4 Support Slots 
4 PvP Slots 
1 Wildcard Slot (any Soul type)

Note: PvP Souls only affect your PvP status and do not influence PvE.

¶ Soul Rarity and Slot Compatibility
There are three types of nodes (slots) in the Soul Tree:

Common : Accepts Common , Rare , and Legendary Souls 
Rare : Accepts Rare and Legendary Souls 
Legendary : Only accepts Legendary Souls 

¶ Drop Locations and Rarity
Currently, there are 101 Souls available from maps ranging from level 01 to map 113 (Abyss of the Sea) .

Common Souls : Basic attributes, easier to obtain.
Rare Souls : Enhanced attributes, harder to drop.
Legendary Souls : Most powerful, rarest drops.

¶ Upgrading Souls
Souls can have levels ranging from 1 to 3 . To upgrade a Soul, you need multiple copies of the same Soul.
For the bonus gained from the soul it only matters what level it is, not how many souls you used in the range of that level.

¶ Example
To level up a Hopy Soul (Common) :

Level 0 to 1: 1 Soul
Level 1 to 2: 24 Souls
Level 2 to 3: 25 Souls
Total: 50 Souls

¶ Modifying Your Fusion Tier
Mistakes happen! You have various options if you’ve made a mistake:
¶ 1. Reset a Specific Node

Click on the node you want to reset.
Press “Reset Node” at the bottom right.
Requires Fusion Node Reset Stone .

¶ How to Obtain:

Boss rolls
Fusionist NPC (Pillai/Ricarten) for 7,425,000 gold 
Item Shop for 50 coins 

¶ 2. Switch Between Pages

Click “One of the pages” at the bottom right of the interface.
Requires Fusion Tier Switch Stone .

¶ How to Obtain:

Item Shop for 150 coins 
Have an active subscription Bronze/Silver/Gold for 2-4-8 weekly free switches .

¶ 3. Reset the Entire Tree

Click “Reset Tree” at the bottom right of the interface.
Requires Fusion Tier Reset Stone .

¶ How to Obtain:

Item Shop for 250 coins 

Tip: Make sure you’re using the correct stone!

Node Reset Stone: Resets a single node
Tier Switch Stone: Switches between pages
Tier Reset Stone: Resets the entire tree

--
¶ Fusion Pages
You want to have multi-setups? Not a problem this is possible through Fusion pages, 5 total are available.

Purchase a Fusion Page from the coin shop (J) for 2000 coin.
Switch to your desired page to utilize your favorite soul setup.
Requires Fusion Tier Switch Stone or have an active subscription for weekly free switches .
